# jamaican-salaries-2017
Data analysis of a survey on salary satisfaction in Jamaica. 

### Attribute information:
* Age
* Gender
* Education Level
* Foreign Education 
* Field
* Experience
* Time in current role
* Rank of current role
* Net monthly salary
* Lunch allowance 
* Vehicle maintenance 
* Clothing allowance 
* Health insurance 
* Cell phone 
* Basic expenses 
* Entertainment 
* Salary knowledge 
* Attitude 
* Salary satisfaction
